Mandatory MFA Rollout Plan

Status
Draft · June 2026
Goal of this plan

Make MFA mandatory for OSCAR Pro Auth users in a controlled, staged way that supports compliance and security requirements — while minimizing clinic disruption, Support volume, and avoidable login issues.

Phase 1
Pre-Launch
1
Readiness & Dependencies

Confirm operational readiness before any external timelines are communicated. All teams aligned on MFA approach, recovery flows, edge cases, and support processes.

  • MFA policy approved by Security, Compliance, Product, Support & Eng
  • Acceptable MFA factors confirmed; recovery flows for edge cases
  • Support runbooks, macros, escalation paths, and FAQs built
  • Reporting requirements and baseline metrics defined
Internal
Phase 2
Early Awareness
2
Soft Announcement → First Formal Announcement

Soft Pendo survey gathers readiness data and flags risk clinics. First formal Pendo + email announcement sets the expectation: MFA is mandatory — enable now to transition on your own timeline.

  • Pendo soft message: MFA is coming, time to prepare
  • Readiness survey: individual emails, shared logins, device access
  • First formal announcement via Pendo + email newsletter
  • Links to MFA setup docs and available factor options
Pendo Email
Phase 3
Voluntary Period
3
Voluntary Enablement & Monitoring

Clinics self-enable MFA at their own pace. Product, Engineering, and Support monitor adoption trends, close runbook gaps, and provide targeted outreach to high-risk or not-yet-enabled clinics.

  • Track MFA enablement by clinic and user
  • Monitor support volume: setup issues, lockouts, resets
  • Adjust docs based on common confusion patterns
  • Targeted outreach to non-enabled or at-risk clinics
Internal
Phase 4
Enforcement Prep
4
Enforcement Date → Final Reminder

Communicate the enforcement date and create urgency. Targeted reminders to non-enabled clinics via Pendo, email, and CS outreach.

  • Communicate enforcement date to users with clear instructions
  • Final targeted outreach to non-enabled clinics and users
  • Identify SMS-only and shared-login risks before go-live
  • Confirm support staffing and exception / rollback process
Pendo Email CS Outreach
Phase 5
Enforcement
5
Mandatory MFA Go-Live

Enable mandatory MFA for all Pro Auth users. Monitor sign-in failures, lockouts, and support volume closely. Escalate quickly. Apply exceptions only through the approved process.

  • Enable MFA requirement per the approved enforcement approach
  • Monitor sign-in failures, lockouts, enrollment issues, tickets
  • Escalate high-severity access issues quickly
  • Apply exception process for approved edge cases only
Engineering Support
Phase 6
Post-Launch
6
Stabilization & Optimization

Monitor post-enforcement metrics for several weeks. Update documentation, normalize support volume, reduce unnecessary SMS usage, and publish a final stakeholder summary with lessons learned.

  • Monitor enrollment, failed sign-ins, resets, and exceptions
  • Review support ticket themes and update documentation
  • Reduce SMS usage toward scalable, lower-cost options
  • Capture lessons learned · Publish final stakeholder summary
Internal
